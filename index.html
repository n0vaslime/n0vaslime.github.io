About me

Hi!

My name is Archie Still (they/them)

I graduated from the University of the West of England in Bristol, with a First in BSc(Hons) Games Technology. I also achieved an award from The Independent Game Developers' Association (TIGA) for 'Outstanding Graduate of the Year in Games Technology', for my work throughout the course.

Video games have been a part of my life for as long as I can remember, and I am especially driven to develop games of my own to give back to this industry. I particularly take interest in video game narratives and how they can be intertwined with gameplay mechanics.

I have experience using Godot, Unity and Unreal, as well as coding using C++, C#, GDScript and Python, specialising particularly in level design. I also have some experience in 3D modelling using Autodesk Maya, and experience in writing game analyses at an academic and professional level, as I have written an academic paper on the representation of asexuality in video games that is currently being reviewed for publication by Game Studies.

I am currently looking into freelance work while developing my own portfolio further, in preparation of taking a Masters degree next year. Please note that I have no intention of working alongside or using generative AI as part of the development process.
